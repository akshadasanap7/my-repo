Who We Are

SB Vegetable Company is a trusted name in the fruit and vegetable industry, based in Nashik, Maharashtra. With a passion for freshness and a commitment to quality, we specialize in preserving and delivering premium produce to homes and businesses across India.

What We Do

From farm to fork, we handle every step with care. Our operations include sourcing, processing, and distributing dried and dehydrated fruits, vegetables, and specialty food products. Whether it’s soup mixes or seasonal staples, we ensure every product meets the highest standards. Our Mission To nourish communities with fresh, sustainable, and affordable produce—while supporting local farmers and promoting healthy living.
